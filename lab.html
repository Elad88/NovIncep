The problem

Most robotics labs do not fail because of bad equipment.

They fail because the learning goals, operating assumptions, simulation workflow, safety model, curriculum integration, maintenance logic, and procurement priorities were not defined clearly enough.

  • Hardware is purchased before the lab operating model is clear.
  • Simulation, physical systems, and student workflows remain disconnected.
  • Safety, supervision, maintenance, and ownership are treated too late.
  • Leadership receives a budget request, not a decision framework.
